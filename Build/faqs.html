Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Goa Table Tennis Association (GTTA)?

The Goa Table Tennis Association is the official body that oversees the promotion and development of table tennis in Goa. We organize events, coaching camps, and competitions for players of all levels.

2. How can I become a member of GTTA?

To become a member, you can fill out the membership application form available on our website or contact us directly through the contact information provided.

3. Are there any age restrictions for participating in events?

No, we encourage players of all ages to participate in our events. We organize categories for different age groups and skill levels.

4. How can I stay updated with GTTA news and events?

You can stay updated by following our website and social media channels. We regularly post news, event schedules, and announcements.

5. Do you provide coaching for beginners?

Yes, GTTA offers coaching programs for beginners. You can find more information about coaching sessions on our website.
